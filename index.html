Why a Latham pool?

Latham is the largest designer, manufacturer and marketer of in-ground residential swimming pools in North America, Australia and New Zealand, with over six decades of experience at the center of the backyard lifestyle. Every day, thousands of families in North America, Europe and Australia enjoy Latham pools and we want you to have the knowledge and confidence to select the one that is right for you.
